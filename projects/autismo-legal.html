Autismo Legal (Legal Autism)
Autismo Legal is an application for parents to monitor their children who have the autistic spectrum, the application has a series of features to block parents about what their legal rights are in relation to their children, in addition to having guidance booklets and invitation to lectures and events directed to these themes. The application also has tests to help the level of the disorder and proposes exercises to develop children's skills. My role in this application was hybrid and designer web development. The technologies used: Vue.js, Adobe XD e Amazon S3
