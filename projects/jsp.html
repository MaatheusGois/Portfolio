UNIVERSITY PRESBITERIAN MACKENZIE
In times of pandemic, in the midst of so much misinformation it is necessary for churches that wish to communicate with their members, a means of direct communication that clearly speaks what should happen in the next days of the program bringing words of comfort and consolation in the midst of Crisis and reminding them that there is a God who takes care of us at all times. In this project I had the honor of working as a Designer using Figma as a prototyping tool.
