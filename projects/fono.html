Creating an Application for Detection of Disorders in Children

MackPesquisa
The child's first years of life are crucial for the proper development of language. It is the responsibility of the speech therapist to seek and identify children with deficits in the development of communication early. This is considered one of the great markers and early indicators of the child's global development.

The early identification of speech-language disorders combined with guidance to family members and caregivers significantly increases the chances of a good prognosis. Although there are specific research and evaluation instruments for the language area, standardized scales and screening and evaluation instruments have proved to be useful and necessary tools, which can contribute to referrals for diagnosis. In this scenario, a good screening instrument must be short, inexpensive, and easy to administer and interpret.

In this way the proposal of this study to develop and validate a digital tool for the early identification of language deficits in a large number of children in early childhood with the possibility of investigating evidence of developmental disorders and determination of children who would benefit from an assessment more complete becomes a priority.
