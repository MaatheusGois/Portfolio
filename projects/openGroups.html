UNIVERSITY PRESBITERIAN MACKENZIE
Since the foundation of Mackenzie in 1870, as a result of the missionary and educational action of the couple George Whitehil and Mary Annesley Chamberlain, the welcoming and spiritual care of the entire Mackenzist community has been part of the expression of the reformed Christian confessionality, which defines Mackenzie's institutional identity and its Lifetime Associate, the Presbyterian Church of Brazil. The website is an access path for new students to get to know the Christian groups at the university.
