What is a dedicated team?

A dedicated team is a team of in-house Exyte engineers that integrates into your company, processes and infrastructure. Your company is responsible for all the management like assigning tasks, planning sprints, daily standups and tracking progress. Dedicated engineers act as your regular employees working remotely from the Exyte office.

Reasons to choose Exyte as your dedicated team

  • You want to quickly extend your team with talented engineers.
  • Your tech department is missing some required expertise and you want to fill it with experienced developers.
  • You want to be in touch with each developer yourself.
  • You don’t want to waste time on HR and administrative routine.
